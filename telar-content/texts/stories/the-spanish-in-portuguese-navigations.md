title: "Portuguese navigations"

European decline after the fall of the Roman Empire. Disconnected from broader world. Henry the navigator. Trade. Black Death. Crusades and Reconquest. Canaries and Antilles. Iberian rivalries. Portuguese navigating to Asia around Africa. Disrupting Venetians previous monopoly omn trades. Concurrent Atlantic crossing. Changing shipping technologies with Latin sails and new weaponry.
